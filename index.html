    About Us

    Khetra is the new way of smart farming and technology innovation & business intelligence with an aim to make farming as a micro entrepreneurship opportunity for the young farmers.

    Our Mission

    To build the most trusted B2B marketplace for unorganized markets allowing retailers to conveniently purchase directly from the source, without the hassle of price negotiations, quality assurance and payment security.

    Our Plan

    Leveraging technology and build a trusted b2b market place where farmers can list their produce with expected price and buyers can source directly from the farmers. Helping farmers reach a wider global market.

    Our Vision

    To be the most trusted sourcing platform for all the retailers in the unorganized markets of the world & to make farming as a micro entrepreneurship opportunity & stop farmers saying "we do this because we have no choice."
